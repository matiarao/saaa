My Travel Bucket List

  • Paris
  • Kyoto
  • Batanes

Paris, France

The City of Lights is known for its art, architecture, and cuisine.

I want to visit the Louvre and try authentic croissants near the Seine.

Paris represents culture, elegance, and inspiration for me.

Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto blends tradition and tranquility, with beautiful shrines and gardens.

I want to see the Fushimi Inari gates and the Arashiyama bamboo grove.

It’s the perfect place to reflect and appreciate nature’s calm.

Batanes, Philippines

Batanes offers scenic landscapes and peaceful living.

I want to see the lighthouses, stone houses, and rolling hills.

It’s one of the best representations of serenity and natural beauty.
